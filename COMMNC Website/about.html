COMMNC is a leading advocate and your #1 source for exclusive and latest news and updates around the gaming community.

COMMNC stands for Cara, Orrin, Mikee, Miguel and Company and we have two objectives: to report on the gaming industry all over the world as well as to be the go-to site for geek fans.

We are always hungry for content and updates, just like you all. So if you have something up - be it a game still under development, upcoming gaming tournament, community events, even those that involve cosplay, comics, or anything related to gaming, reach out to us! We want to know what you got, and spread the exciting news!

Do you have intriguing ideas for events and content? Or suggestions and recommendations as to how we can improve? Hit us up in our email ad and we’ll gladly respond to you! For more information, check out our Contact Us.
